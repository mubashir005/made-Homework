# Climate Change Impact on Planet Earth's Temperature

## Description

Climate change is one of the most pressing issues of our time, significantly affecting global temperatures and weather patterns. This project aims to explore the correlation between CO2 emissions per capita and changes in average global temperatures over the years. By analyzing historical data on CO2 emissions and global temperatures, we aim to gain insights into the relationship between human activities and climate change.

## Main Questions

1. How does the trend in CO2 emissions per capita correlate with changes in average global temperature over the years?
2. Does climate change play any role in the rise of Earth's temperature?
3. What types of sources produce the most CO2 per capita?
